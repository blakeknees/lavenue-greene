About Us

Learn more about the creators behind the brands.

Brian Kierans

I’m a cannabis patient and cannabis nerd.

I’ve used cannabis medically all my adult life to treat chronic illness. I judged the Karma Cup from 2014 through 2016.

My edibles brand Dovercourt Park Bakery competed in The Karma Cup in the edible category in 2015 and 2016. My signature product Cannasyrup won an award from Lift & Co. in 2016.

I was a regular competitor in the Mastah Concentrate series from its inception in 2016, through 2018. I collaborated with Feels Like More on Buttermilk Pie ft Corn Cookie Crust, we won the May 2018 Mastah Edibles Series.

In 2019, I co-hosted Vapor Central 420 celebrations with dab bar, edibles, door prizes and DJs.

I grow cannabis indoor and outdoor which I have processed into live resin and kief. I dab upwards of 1g of extract everyday, I make & take cannabis oils orally before bed and use them topically frequently.

I live with Jennifer and our two dogs in Hamilton. I enjoy Star Wars, Star Trek, Marvel content- I (still) play Pokémon & Fortnite everyday.

Jennifer Blakney

I am a front-end developer, writer, pastry chef, and cannabis educator living in Hamilton.

In 2016 I submitted my lemon macaron to medical cannabis competition The Karma Cup and won 2nd place in the Edibles category.

In 2019 I participated at Full Tilt Conference by speaking on the 'Cannabis Country' panel alongside other industry professionals on the topic of current edibles policy.

I've been a freelance writer and editor for the Ontario Cannabis Store since 2021, and have written for numerous legal brands as well.

When I'm not expanding my coding knowledge, I’m usually cooking, baking, walking my two dogs, or watching television with my partner.
