Canna Syrup

Cannasyrup is Dovercourt Park Bakery’s 2016 Lift & Co. Canadian Cannabis Awards winner: Cannasyrup is made with fresh fruit and cannabis extract. Great with sparkling water, soothing tea or your favourite savoury dish.

Flavours include Cranberry, Lemon Ginger, Mango Ginger, Citrus and Grapefruit.

The Higher High

Founded in 2017 by both Brian Kierans and Jennifer Blakney, The Higher High is a cannabis-infused intimacy oil that values self-worth and self-exploration. Marketing will always be non-gendered, education focused and mainly geared towards those within LGBTQ2SIA communities. The goal is to encourage users to find new ways of integrating cannabis and intimacy into their lives.

Example: to be used before, during, or after bathing. Already prepped for adult and medical market, packaging is left intentionally plain with little colouring.

Products have been extensively tested on both cannabis novices and the experienced. Final formulas will include THC, CBD, and 1:1 THC/CBD to encapsulate various experiences and comfort levels.

Products are food safe, but not latex safe.

Dovercourt Park Bakery

Never satisfied with the quality of edibles made by others, Brian Kierans founded Dovercourt Park Bakery in 2015 as a response to his consumer experiences. The brand is dedicated to providing a quality experience for both cannabis newcomers and aficionados.

Dovercourt Park Bakery is thoughtful, flavourful, and wholesome. Only ethically sourced chocolates are used, as well as natural dyes and colours. Recipes have been under extensive development, testing with and without cannabis to achieve the perfect balance of richness and flavour. Inspiration is derived from classic,mid-20th century North American recipes and flavours. and uses the theme of nostalgia to create original products that appeal to a new market. Recipes are protected under intellectual property laws as Trade Secrets. This allows for protection indefinitely.

Terp Cream

Developed in 2018 by Brian Kierans, Terp Cream is a series of whimsical ice cream treats flavoured with botanically-derived cannabis terpenes. This product is meant to highlight the complex aromatic gustatory that cannabis possesses, without any effects.

Current profiles include: Grand Daddy Purple, Lemon Haze, Tahoe OG, OG Kush.

Any strain of cannabis that can be profiled for terpenes can be churned into Terp Cream. Terp Cream's deep flavour profiles rival and surpass those of expensive vanilla beans at much lower price point.

Feels Like More

Founded in 2015 by Jennifer Blakney, Feels Like More is intended for special occasions.

Feels Like More adapts traditional European indulgences inspired by Blakney’s pastry training at Le Cordon Bleu. Products include macarons, decorated cakes of all sizes, petit fours, entremets, and chocolates.

Recipes have been under extensive development, testing with and without cannabis to achieve the perfect balance of richness and flavour. Products have also been exposed to a variety of environmental conditions for shelf life evaluation.

All recipes are proprietary, utilizing only the highest quality ingredients.
